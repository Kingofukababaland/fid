Volunteer With Us

Volunteers are an important part of Green Paw Sanctuary. They help animals feel safe, loved, and comfortable while they wait for adoption.

Volunteer Jobs

  1. Walk dogs and help them exercise.
  2. Clean animal rooms and play areas.
  3. Help at adoption events.
  4. Take photos of pets for the website.

Time Needed

Most volunteers help for two or three hours each week.
