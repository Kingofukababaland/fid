About Our Organization

Green Paw Sanctuary was created as a peaceful rescue center for animals that need patient care and a safe home. Our goal is to support pets until they are ready to join a family.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, protect, and rehome animals while teaching the community about kindness and responsible pet ownership.

What We Believe

  • Every animal deserves respect.
  • Adoption should be thoughtful and responsible.
  • Community support can change lives.

Quick Facts

Founded: 2026

Location: Central Florida

Focus: Rescue, adoption, and education
