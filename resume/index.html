About Me

Hello, my name is Kingston Steele. I am a student at Seminole State and an aspiring artist & animator. My interest in art began when I was very young and has continued to grow over the years. Growing up with western cartoons has had a great influence on my style and art philosophies. As I honed my skills over the years, my talents spread towards digital art & animation.
